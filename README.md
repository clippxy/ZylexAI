## 🧠 What is NexusAI?

NexusAI is a web-based AI automation suite that gives users access to powerful AI tools — from summarizing text and generating titles, to drafting emails and planning full projects. Powered by [Claude AI](https://anthropic.com) from Anthropic.

Built for people who move fast. No login. No setup. Just results.
